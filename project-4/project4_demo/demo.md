# Demo (Beta) Version for Project 4 - Resource Management

## Mechanics
- Move around the environment with WASD
- E for collecting items, opening puzzles
- Space for fighting enemies throughout the game
- Drag-and-drop UI for burning items (increases time)
- Offer tickets to statues in correct order

## Gameplay
- Move through variants of the environment, solving puzzles, collecting items, and finding information on the order to offer items to the statues
- Watch time and manage resources effectively, including the burning of items, in order to move throughout all variants and collect the last ticket
- Battle against enemies (Episode 2) and use space key to fight
- Implements same core structure from Episodes 2 and 3, as a 2D pixel game in Godot with basic movement
