
# Service Suspended

An interactive narrative game playing as a setup as a part of a 4 part series of how your mundane life gets thrown in a **loop** when you find a ominous ticket on the floor. What is the ticket about? Why am I living through the same day? All will be revealed in due time...

# Game 1: Interactive Narrative

## Setup

**Source File:**
1. Install Twine from https://twinery.org/
2. Import the story file from `src/`
3. Open the story labelled "Service Suspended"
4. Navigate to the "Build" tab on the top
5. Click "Play"
6. Interactive Narrative should open in your browser
7. Enjoy!

**HTML File:**
1. Open the story file file from `docs/`
2. Open the file labelled "Service Suspended.html"
3. Interactive Narrative should open in your browser
4. Enjoy!

## Team Members
Eswar Talasila, Zachary Boston, Mokshagna Kadiyala, Nishil Rally

## Story Format
Harlowe 3.3.9

## Controls
Mouse to scroll and click choice options

## Choice Suggestions
All choices lead to the same ending as this game is a setup for the coming games, however we encourage you to be as curious as possible to gain as much knowledge as possible.

# Game 2: Dungeon Crawler

## Setup

**Source File:**
1. Install Godot Engine (version 3.5 or later recommended).
2. Open Godot and click “Import”.
3. Select the project folder from src/ labelled DungeonCrawler (project2).
4. Open the project.
5. Click Play Scene or press F5 to start the game.

## Team Members
Eswar Talasila, Zachary Boston, Mokshagna Kadiyala, Nishil Rally

## Story Format
Pixel-style dungeon exploration with interactive combat and ticket collection mechanics.

## Controls
Move with WASD
Punch with Spacebar
Open Inventory / Collect Ticket / Collect Health with	E
Pause / Menu

## Gameplay Features
1. Ticket Punching: Collect tickets scattered throughout each dungeon floor.
2. Combat: Fight enemies using spacebar. Punch enemies to reduce their health.
3. Obstacles: Avoid spikes and hazards. Timing and positioning are key.
4. Levels: Explore dungeon floors and loop through to change position in dungeon.
5. Health and Resources: Manage your health and energy while fighting enemies and navigating obstacles.

## Tips for Players
1. Explore every corner; some tickets are hidden in chests and others are in extreme corners of the dungeon.
2. Watch enemy patterns to avoid taking damage unnecessarily.
3. Punching tickets and defeating enemies helps to complete the game, but survival is key.
4. Interacting with the environment (doors, levers, traps) may help to escape.
